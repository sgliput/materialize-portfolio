Sam Liput

Full-stack Web Developer and Professional Knowledge Seeker
My name is Sam Liput, friend, poet, and web developer.

After some years in the customer service world, I decided to broach the IT industry and make a new career out of it.

To make that happen, I completed a Web Design certificate program at Reynolds Community College in Richmond, VA, and seeing even more room to grow, expanded those lessons further with the University of Richmond Coding Boot Camp.

Trained in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, MySQL, MongoDB, and React.js, I have endeavored to foster the skills needed to succeed in this constantly evolving industry.

I love puzzles, especially those written in code, and I welcome the satisfaction that comes from overcoming a challenge. (Seriously, it makes all the headaches worth it!)

Comfortable with the visual side of front-end or the logical side of back-end
